Hi, my name is Philip Moak.

I'm a UI Designer from Los Angeles.

My name is Philip Moak, I graduated from the University of Colorado in Boulder in 2008 with a BA in Film Studies. After working four years in the film industry I developed a passion for music production and songwriting. I didn’t want to make this my career focus but I knew I wanted a job that was creative and paid the bills, so I became a hairstylist.

While I loved my time working as a hairstylist I found myself growing tired of the chaos of the beauty industry and decided that it was time for a change. I started looking more into UI design and it felt like the perfect next step. As I began studying UI I realized how similar it was to hair design, and while the final product may be different, the design process does follow similar patterns.

When I’m not designing I love to spend my time in the outdoors, hiking and camping with my friends. Music production is still a strong passion of mine so I also devote time writing and recording songs for my personal music project. I also have an interest in astrology, tarot, and all things metaphysical.
